The Journey is a project I started because I really wanted to make a game all by myself. I wanted the game to be about fear and show how you can overcome fear by confronting yourself with that fear. I did this by using light as a way to express fear. The main character is a boy who is afraid of the dark. If he’s out of the light for too long, he’ll be too afraid and has to restart the level. Through the levels he meets different people, each with their own fear. He helps them overcoming their fear and that way he learns how to get out of the world he’s been teleported into. This project was a great learning experience for me. I posted my progress on Instagram and as you can see, there's a lot that has changed throughout the process.
