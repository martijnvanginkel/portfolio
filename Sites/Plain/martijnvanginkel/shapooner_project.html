Together with some other game-designers, we decided to all make a game within one day. The only rule being that a ‘ball’ had to be included somewhere in your game. Shapooner is a game where you need to fit into different shapes floating in space by tapping on your phone. Making everything black and white and adding spacy-music with some effects. Later I added a menu and called it “Shapooner”.
